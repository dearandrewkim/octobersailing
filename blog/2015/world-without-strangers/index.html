World without strangers

Hello to my childhood friends and to strangers living on the other side of the planet. Welcome to October Sailing, my n-th attempt at blogging.

By following October Sailing, you will be boarding on my leaky (yet cosy) boat and joining me on my figure-out-the-meaning-of-life-love-and-everything-else journey through seas of random thoughts. So before you take the risk, it might be best to know you a little bit about me, your captain.

My name is Andrew Kim. I am a designer and nerd at heart and a very competent bossy-pants. I love starting side projects with friends or by myself (Check out Darganist, Urban Pancakes, Winter Sea, Dear Andrew Kim). I graduated from Bachelor of Engineering Honours and Bachelor of Design in Architecture at the University of Sydney at the end of 2014. I am currently working in the field of Project Management and Sustainability for an Australian culturally significant building (see my linkedin to find out which building).

Here, I hope to write original content about design, architecture, engineering, sustainability, or anything which I think is worth blogging about. Writing a blog will help me think more deeply about the issues, clarify my thoughts and improve my writing skills.
