Navigating through my own sea of thoughts

26 September 16

Positive Thinking

  • I will meet my own expectation and not others.
  • I will control my own spiritual journey.
  • I will only do activities which are in line with my values.
  • I will be strong throughout all difficult times.
  • I will set clear boundaries and ask others to respect them.
  • I will be clear to myself about what I am willing to compromise.
  • I will take care of my own happiness.
  • I will fight for things worth fighting for.
  • I will be nonjudgemental
  • I will always be grateful for things others have done for me.
  • I will be proud to be who I am.
  • I will remind myself that I am a good person with good intention.
  • I will be realistic.
