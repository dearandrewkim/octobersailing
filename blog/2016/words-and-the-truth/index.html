Words and the truth

You say you're sorry for everything, but what does this even mean? It's a blanket statement, it doesn't address the root of the problem nor does it help you to change.

You always seem to forget your intent of your apology. And people only forget things if it had no meaning to them.

But am I being a hypocrite? Do I say things that I don't mean?

If my words and my heart don't align, how can I change how I feel? Or, how should I say how I really feel?

We've been friends for a long time but that doesn't mean that I'm immune by all the hurtful things that you've done. I'm still hurt by you. And I've hurt you too I know.

I can pretend like there's nothing wrong. But I think that we obth change and when we're both ready, we can be friends again.
