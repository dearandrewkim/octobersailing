21 August 16

I can't always be the good guy

I can't always be the good guy.

Sometimes, I need to put myself first instead of other people's feeling. Sometimes, I need to deal with my own problem not others.

I have the right to feel my emotions. I have the right to take my time.

I will not compromise on these two things and I won't apologise.
